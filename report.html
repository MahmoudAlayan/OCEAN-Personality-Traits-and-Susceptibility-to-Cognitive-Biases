Evidence-based debiasing tips

  • Generate counter-explanations and look for disconfirming information before deciding.
  • Use numeric anchors only as starting points; deliberately estimate without them first.
  • Slow down high-stakes judgments and check for alternative interpretations.
  • Seek structured feedback and compare against objective benchmarks when available.
