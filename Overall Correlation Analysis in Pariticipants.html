    Methodological notes and limitations

    This analysis is exploratory, correlational, and based on a convenience sample. Correlations do not imply causation, and effect sizes may shift with broader or more diverse samples. Measures are brief, and the bias tasks are experimental; results should be interpreted as descriptive patterns rather than definitive psychological traits.
