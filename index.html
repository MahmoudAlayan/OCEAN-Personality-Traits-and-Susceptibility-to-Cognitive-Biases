Informed consent

This project explores whether Big Five traits predict susceptibility to anchoring and confirmation biases. Cognitive bias tasks

This project explores whether Big Five traits predict susceptibility to anchoring and confirmation biases. You will complete a brief personality inventory, engage in two bias tasks, and then view a statistical report based on your responses - It is not a diagnostic tool and does not provide medical or clinical advice.

Anchoring task

For each item, first decide whether the true value is higher or lower than the anchor. Then provide your best estimate.

Confirmation-bias task

Read each evidence snippet and rate how strong it seems. The snippets are balanced and non-political.

Fill plausible responses to preview the report quickly.
